Refugees crisis supported by
Serendipity arts grants for Theatre.

The project is aimed to understand the condition of humanity in the global issue of refugee crisis for example in the refugee camps, in the journey of finding a place to stay, their state of mind leaving everything behind belonging to them, their fears and death of loved ones and the deep values of human rights and freedom when one is forced to migrate or internally displaced. And we as global citizens understand its causes?
