About Akhoka

   The group Akhoka is a collective of artists from different disciplines that aims to create critical physical performances and other allied activities of arts through a collaborative approach. We mainly like to address and question our socio, economic and political problems through performance. Akhoka is also interested in community-led and community-engaged performances by entering into different spaces of communities resulting from conflict, speedy urbanization, ills of social-economic and political institutions, displacement from man-made catastrophes, etc. We are also seeking an alternative and sustainable environment for practicing arts that affect the process and production. At the same time, we also want to consistently push the boundaries of our own practice and the boundaries of the field in which we are working by resonating, embodying, and questioning the contents to create critical performance praxis.

   Akhoka believes in the inclusive arts practice therefore we welcome you all irrespective of your discipline or background to join and helps us for fostering a better vision and praxis through the creative medium of performance and other allied activities of arts.

Our Team

Victor Thoudam

Artistic Director

Victor Thoudam did his Bachelor in physics honors from University of Delhi in 2007. He studied acting at the National School of Drama (NSD), New Delhi from 2009-2012. He was awarded the Charles Wallace India Trust Scholarship and went to London International School of performing Arts, (LISPA), 2015-16, now Arthaus Berlin. He seeks for a consistent practice of preparing the body and mind rigorously to understand different psychophysical aspects and the performance space as an inclusive-ensemble between the artistes and the audience. His work questioned and analyzed the relationship between the practice of theatre and the deep hidden social reality. He is also the recipient of Serendipity Arts Grant for theatre 2020-21.

Tom Maisnam

Sound designer/ Editor

Tom Maisnam is a sound designer and editor. He has been composing music and designing sound for Akhoka Theatre since 2018. He is also well known in the Manipuri film industry and theatre circle for sound design and video editing. He is also a guitarist at ‘The Dirty Strikes’.

Rajpritam Loushigam

Artist

Rajpritam did his graduation from the Manipur University of Culture in theatre in 2020. He is currently pursuing his Master degree in the same university. He participated many theatre workshops and festivals in the national and international levels.
